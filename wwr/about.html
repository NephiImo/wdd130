History

White Water Rafting has been around for centuries. It is believed that the first recorded instance of White Water Rafting was in 1811 when the first recorded descent of the Snake River in Wyoming was made. Since then, White Water Rafting has become a popular sport around the world. Today, there are many different rivers that are perfect for White Water Rafting, including the Colorado River, the Zambezi River, and the Futaleufu River. No matter where you are in the world, there is sure to be a river that is perfect for White Water Rafting.
